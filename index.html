Bank Credit Risk Reporting System

Project Overview

Spearheaded the design and development of an enterprise-wide credit risk reporting system for a major financial institution, addressing growing demands for risk transparency, regulatory compliance, and executive decision-making.

Key Responsibilities

  • Designed automated reporting workflows replacing manual processes.
  • Developed ETL pipelines for integrating multi-source financial data.
  • Implemented real-time risk dashboards for executive decision-making.
  • Collaborated with compliance teams to ensure Basel III & IFRS 9 alignment.
  • Optimized data quality frameworks improving accuracy by 35%.

Challenges

  • Data silos across multiple banking systems created integration issues.
  • Legacy infrastructure limited scalability and performance.
  • Regulatory requirements demanded audit-ready traceability.

Solutions Implemented

Centralized Data Warehouse: Consolidated credit risk exposure, default probabilities, and capital adequacy metrics.
Regulatory Compliance Automation: Built templates for IFRS 9 staging and Basel III reporting.
Visualization & Insights: Designed dashboards using Power BI for executive leadership.
Tech Stack: Python SQL Server Power BI ETL Pipelines Excel VBA
